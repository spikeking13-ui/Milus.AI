# Milus AI
Problem: Elderly Loneliness





Loneliness among older adults is a growing public health crisis.



88.57% of elderly participants report moderate-to-severe loneliness.



Chronic loneliness is linked to:





Depression



Cognitive decline



Increased mortality risk



Families cannot monitor emotional wellbeing daily.



Existing technology is often too complex for elderly users.







Our Solution: Milus.AI





Voice-forward AI companion for older adults



Provides:





Daily conversation



Emotional monitoring



Memory-based engagement



Caregiver insights



Goal: Enhance real-world human connection, not replace it



Users spend ~30 minutes/day talking about:





Family



Hobbies



Life stories



AI uses these conversations to help family members stay informed and connected
