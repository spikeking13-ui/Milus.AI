Milus.AI — Addressing Elderly Loneliness
The Problem

Loneliness among older adults is a growing public health crisis:

88.57% of elderly participants report moderate-to-severe loneliness

Chronic loneliness is linked to:

Depression

Cognitive decline

Increased mortality risk

Families often cannot monitor emotional wellbeing daily

Existing technology is often too complex for elderly users

Our Solution

Milus.AI is a voice-first AI companion designed for older adults.

It provides:

Daily conversation and emotional support

Memory-based engagement for personalized interactions

Insights for caregivers to monitor wellbeing

Goal: Enhance real-world human connection, not replace it.

Users spend ~30 minutes per day talking about:

Family

Hobbies

Life stories

Milus uses these conversations to help family members stay informed and connected.
